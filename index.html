Forest Park Youth Soccer Association

FPYSA is an independent organization dedicated to motivating kids and teens in the Forest Park community to get active and live healthy lives.
It runs both a Spring and Fall league comprised of 8 weeks of games each.
They utilize the Park District of Forest Park's state of the art soccer field for practices and games during the soccer season.
In both of these seasons, they engage other local townships in order to increase the competition and afford more game opportunities to the participating children.

Obstacles Faced

FPYSA is completely independent from the Forest Park Park District.

This meant that raising funds for the program wass the complete responsibility of the Association.

FPYSA had to work hard to start the league...

and keep it going.

This proved to be a it of an obstacle.

They had to keep the program affordable for the community, all while making enough money to continue the program.

They worked around this and now the afforable soccer program is flourishing!

The program has been established for years now and is continuing on stronger than ever.

Why FPSA?

I played on the FPYSA Varsity soccer team for four seasons.

The program not only helped me to discover my love for the sport and grow as a player,
it also helped to develop me into the person I am today.

My coaches and team mates helped me to develop the necessary soft skills required in life.
They helped me to bloom socially and made me step out of my comfort zone consistantly.

I have refed for the program for three years now,
and I have voluntered as the Little Kickers' assistant coach for the past two seasons.

I have a deep need to give back, and FPYSA is happy to allow me to do so.
